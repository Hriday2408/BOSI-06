The philosophy
Answer before
you act.

BOSI-6 is built on a single principle: no action is taken without first answering six questions. The 5W1H framework isn't process overhead — it's a mindset that separates builders who copy from seekers who understand.

The 6 questions
Who
Who are we building for? Who is involved? Who evaluates?
What
What are we building? What is the problem? What is the deliverable?
Where
Where does this happen? Where are the resources?
When
When is the deadline? When does each phase begin?
Why
Why does this matter? Why this approach over another?
How
How do we execute? How do we measure success?
The origin — BlackBosi

BOSI-6 began with a pair of Bosi Tools nose pliers used in Office Container 6 (OC-6) — the space where the founding team first built together. The plier was spray-painted black and became BlackBosi, the mascot. The number 6 turned out to encode everything: 6 questions, 6 domains, 6 seeker tiers.

Evaluation system
Robotic
Knowledge Scale.

RKS is a single number — from 0 to 1 — that represents the area of a hexagon formed by a seeker's scores across 6 domains. It encodes a philosophy: breadth is not optional. Neglecting any domain shrinks adjacent areas simultaneously.

RKS = [ (M+1)(E+1) + (E+1)(C+1) + (C+1)(T+1) + (T+1)(D+1) + (D+1)(S+1) + (S+1)(M+1) ] / 726

Pen-and-paper form: add 1 to each score, multiply adjacent pairs in the hexagon cycle M→E→C→T→D→S→M, sum all 6, divide by 726.

Why do you want to be a Seeker?

Applications for the 2026–27 cohort open in August. Submitting now adds you to the early interest list.

Who can apply?
Any student — from IIT Jammu or any other institute. No prior robotics experience required. Entry is always at Tier 1. What matters is that you show up and answer the six questions.
What happens after you apply?
You'll be invited to an orientation in August where the pipeline, RKS system, and track structure are explained. From there you self-select into tracks and begin accumulating your RKS score.
What does the Triathlon require?
The 48-hour Triathlon in March selects 10–12 seekers who have reached a tier threshold through the year's events. Selection is by RKS standing or direct invite from the council. It is the hardest thing BOSI-6 does.
For faculty and institutes
BOSI-6 operates as a student society reinforced by faculty. We are seeking a faculty advisor and institutional recognition through I2EDC. Contact the council directly for the full proposal document.
